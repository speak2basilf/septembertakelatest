Non-Refundable Fee & Payment Policy

At CliniGlobal, we provide specialized training in Clinical Research, Clinical SAS, and Medical Coding with placement assistance. To maintain clarity and fairness, we follow a strict Non-Refundable Fee Policy. By enrolling in our programs, students acknowledge and agree to the following terms:

Important: All fees paid to CliniGlobal are strictly non-refundable and non-transferable once admission is confirmed.

1. Non-Refundable Payments

All course fees, registration fees, and administrative charges paid to CliniGlobal are strictly non-refundable and non-transferable. Once admission is confirmed or classes have commenced, no refunds will be provided under any circumstances.

2. Course Fee & Enrollment Policy

Students must successfully complete the initial installment payment or finalize the loan process before the commencement of the academic batch. Enrollment rules and admission timelines must be followed, failing which admission will be revoked without refund.

3. Loan & Financial Agreement

Students opting for education loans through our Loan Partner must comply with their terms. By applying, students accept CliniGlobal's non-refundable policy.

4. Program Cancellation by CliniGlobal

If CliniGlobal cancels a program, students are entitled to a 100% refund. Otherwise, no refunds are entertained.

Note: This is the only circumstance under which refunds will be provided.

5. Placement Assistance Policy

Placement assistance is a support service and not a guaranteed job offer. No refunds will be given for dissatisfaction with placement opportunities.

6. Legal Acknowledgment

This Non-Refundable Policy is legally binding. Students are advised to seek legal or financial advice before proceeding with payments or loan agreements.

All disputes will be subject to the jurisdiction of courts in Hyderabad, Telangana.
